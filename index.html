Biography:

I am Nicholas Trilone, the creator of this website, and a Web Dev 1 student. I took this course to both fill a computer elective need, as well as to expand my abilties(after all, who doesn't need a website?). Even if you don't need a website, understanding how they function can be plenty useful in everyday life, especially when working with computers for a living.

I am in my final semester of Raritan Valley Community College, working towards an Associate of Applied Science in Computer Programming. After this semester I plan on working for awhile before continuing my education(at least a bachelors degree in computer science is my goal).

I plan on continuing my pursuit of programming and computer software engineering, regardless of what I end up doing in life. I've been interested in computers since I was young, so focusing on it as either as a career, or as a hobby makes the most sense to me.

I spend my free time primarily playing video games with and going out with friends. Recently, I've been able to spend a lot more time with my friend groups in real life, as disposable income and transport have become more accessible, and pandemic concerns are shrinking.

I tend to like roguelikes/roguelites, horror(especially psychological), and strategy games, although I am willing to play most genres(especially if they are co-op/multiplayer).

I love animals, and have a particular soft spot for cats. My household has three cats, named Lizzy(my cat), Oreo, and Casper, who are all pictured below.

I have an older brother, and three younger half siblings(two brothers and one sister). I currently live at home with my father and brother.
